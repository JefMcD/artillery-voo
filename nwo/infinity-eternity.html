Infinity Eternity

Lost, drifting, forever disconnected, never returning, silly sailing through the endless void, flesh ravaged by their own eco-system, eternally suspended while nature cycles. Here on Earth the leaves fall and flowers tire in their Autumn days and sleep. Again reborn in the Spring, their leaves and blooms roaring with laughter once again under Summers sun. All souls return and we ride around the Sun. It's just a Ride
